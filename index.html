our story

Haliya, a celebrated restaurant, intertwines tradition with modernity. Named after a mythical figure symbolizing strength and hospitality, it aims to provide exceptional service.
The interior merges contemporary design with traditional elements, creating a warm atmosphere. Known for its innovative takes on local recipes, Haliya uses fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring sustainable and delicious dishes. The restaurant's signature offerings are artfully presented, highlighting meticulous culinary craftsmanship. Haliya also engages in community events, promoting local culture and arts. This commitment to excellence has earned it numerous accolades and a loyal following, making it a must-visit dining destination.
